# FUTURE_VISION.md

**Last Updated:** May 28, 2026

Project Genesis aims to become a deep scientific creature stewardship and genetics management simulation.

The strongest current identity is not simply breeding creatures, but managing life across generations inside a failing sanctuary.

## Core Pillars

- predictive genetics
- lineage and inheritance
- creature stewardship
- sanctuary operations
- biological experimentation
- moral consequence
- scientific discovery

## Ultimate Fantasy

A living sanctuary where every generation matters.

The player should feel like:
- caretaker
- scientist
- archivist
- geneticist
- last steward of endangered life

## UI Fantasy

The interface should feel like a scientific breeding terminal:
- clinical
- dense
- readable
- modular
- data-rich
- emotionally restrained but narratively charged

## Long-Term Player Loop

Breed creature
→ inspect traits
→ compare lineage
→ project offspring
→ preserve or risk mutation
→ assign to habitat
→ monitor survival
→ document discoveries
→ continue the bloodline
